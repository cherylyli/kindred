    What is Kindred? How to use Kindred
    Kindred is an online platform that provides unique and interactive travel-related content curated by a community of like-minded world explorers and personalized through tags determined by each user's travelling style.

    1. Find your Travel-Style tags

    2. "Match Me!" to find others who share your taste in traveling OR "Find a place" to discover tourist attractions as recommended by others like you.

    3. Click on profile to read about others' travel stories.
