    What is Kindred? How to use Kindred
          Kindred is an online platform that provides unique and interactive travel-related content curated by a community of like-minded world explorers and personalized through tags determined by each user's travelling style.
    1. Define your preferences or take our quiz
    2. Get matched and narrow your search with our filters
    3. Follow and read about others' travel stories
    Our Mission
          Our mission is to simplify your search for travel destinations, to minimize your screen time and maximize your experience in the real world.
